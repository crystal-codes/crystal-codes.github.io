My Work

LIBRAR-e

Designed as part of the CONNECT: EnAbling Change Post-Secondary Design Competition 2015, hosted by Design Exchange. LIBRAR-e is a mobile app designed to bring library services to mobile devices.

Auntie's Noodle Shop

Auntie's Noodle Shop is visual branding project for a hypothetical restaurant. We created logos, designed print material and menus, and developed a visual story that was based on our own childhood comfort foods.

Bye Bye Meltdown

First created during Hacking Health @ Sick Kids 2014. Bye Bye Meltdown is a healthcare app based on the beaker analogy used to help children manage their stress and prevent meltdowns.
